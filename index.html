About Me

I am a researcher bridging neuroscience and psychology, with a particular focus on the neural mechanisms underlying psychedelic effects. I have led significant research, including a pioneering neuroimaging trial in Australia. My interdisciplinary work combines neuroimaging modalities such as fMRI and EEG to provide insights into brain connectivity and the effects of psychedelics on consciousness.

My goal is to apply my findings to both basic neuroscience and clinical applications, advancing our understanding of brain connectivity and subjective experiences.
