About Me

I am a researcher bridging neuroscience and psychology, specializing in the neural mechanisms underlying psychedelic effects. I lead pioneering studies, including a landmark neuroimaging trial in Australia, using neuroimaging modalities such as fMRI and EEG to deepen our understanding of brain connectivity and consciousness.

My goal is to advance both basic neuroscience and clinical applications, paving the way for enhanced understanding of brain function and subjective experiences.
